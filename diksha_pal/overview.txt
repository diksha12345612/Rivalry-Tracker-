PROJECT NAME: Rivalry Tracker

CATEGORY: Web Development - Online Gaming Website (Fenrir Project)

OVERVIEW

Rivalry Tracker is a web platform built for friends, siblings, roommates, and gaming groups who play multiple games together and want a permanent record of who's actually winning their ongoing rivalry. Instead of bragging rights being based on memory or disputes after each session, Rivalry Tracker keeps a persistent, automatically updated scorecard across every game and every match, year after year.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Casual gaming groups often play a mix of games - FIFA, chess, Mario Kart, COD, carrom, etc. but have no system to track overall standings across all of them. Scores are forgotten, "who's winning" becomes a constant argument, and there's no historical record of past matchups or memorable wins.

SOLUTION

A simple, lightweight web app where users create a "Rivalry" (a group of 2 or more people), add the games they want to track, log match results as they happen, and instantly see an all time scorecard total wins, per-game breakdowns, current win streaks, and a full match history with optional comments/trash talk.

TARGET USERS

Friend groups, siblings, roommates, and couples who regularly play games together and enjoy ongoing competitive banter.

CORE FEATURES (MVP)

- User registration and login
- Create or join a Rivalry group (2+ members)
- Add/manage games tracked within a rivalry
- Log match results (winner, game, date, optional notes)
- Auto-calculated overall scorecard (total wins, per-game wins)
- Win streak tracking
- Match history feed with comments

TECH STACK

MERN (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js)

UNIQUE VALUE PROPOSITION

Unlike gaming stores, review sites, or esports tournament platforms, Rivalry Tracker focuses on the personal, long-term rivalry between specific people - turning everyday gaming sessions into an ongoing competitive story.
